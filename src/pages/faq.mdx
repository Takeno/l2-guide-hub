# Frequently Asked Questions

## Conquest

### How to obtain Seed of Fire?
- Enhancing [Life Source](/guides/conquest#life-source) conquest ability
- Hunting monsters in conquest (slow)
- [Collecting flowers](/guides/conquest#collecting-flowers) (slow)

## Stats

### How to increase Vitality bonus?
- [Life Source](/guides/conquest#life-source) conquest ability
- [Vitalizing](/references/forgotten-power#vitalizing) forgotten ability

### How to increase XP/SP bonus?
- [Life Source](/guides/conquest#life-source) conquest ability
- [Vitalizing](/references/forgotten-power#vitalizing) forgotten ability

### How to increase Attribute Attack?
- [Fire Source](/guides/conquest#fire-source) conquest ability
- [Elemental Guard](/references/forgotten-power#elemental-guard) forgotten ability
- [Opal](/references/jewels#opal) jewel
- [Pa'Agrio Soul Crystal](/references/soul-crystals#paagrios-soul-crystal) on gloves and footwear
- [Talisman of Seven Signs and Epic](/references/talismans#talisman-of-seven-signs-and-epic)

### How to increase Attribute Resistance?
- [Fire Source](/guides/conquest#fire-source) conquest ability
- [Elemental Guard](/references/forgotten-power#elemental-guard) forgotten ability
- [Greater Opal](/references/jewels#opal) jewel
- [Pa'Agrio Soul Crystal](/references/soul-crystals#paagrios-soul-crystal) on lower armor and shield/sigil
- [Talisman of Seven Signs and Epic](/references/talismans#talisman-of-seven-signs-and-epic)
