# Talismans

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  This article is not completed yet.
</Callout>

Talismans in Lineage 2 are powerful artifacts that bestow unique bonuses and enhancements to your character.\
You can equip up to 6 talismans and you need a [Bracelet](#bracelets) with available slots.

Effects of two identical talisman do not stack, the higher-level talisman takes precedence.

## Seed Talismans

There are 7 types of seed talisman. Effects of two seed talismans do not stack, the higher-level talisman takes precedence.

From *Talisman of Infinity* to *Talisman of Insanity* they can be enhanced talking to Seed Talisman Manager. The enhance costs adena and specific items that can be bought from Seed Talisman Manager or Grocery.

|Attempting to get|Chance|Materials*|
|--|--|--|
|Talisman of Destruction|100%|Talisman of Infinity<br />36'000'000 Adena|
|Talisman of Annihilation|50%|Talisman of Destruction<br />4'500'000 Adena<br />1x Energy of Destruction<br />15x Giant's Energy|
|Talisman of Hellfire|40%|Talisman of Annihilation<br />Adena 9'000'000 Adena<br />1x Annihilation Energy<br />30x Giant's Energy|
|Talisman of Desire|30%|Talisman of Hellfire<br />18'000'000 Adena<br />1x Hellfire Energy<br />45x Giant's Energy|
|Talisman of Longing|20%|Talisman of Desire<br />36'000'000 Adena<br />1x Energy of Desire<br />60x Giant's Energy|
|Talisman of Insanity|10%|Talisman of Longing<br />72'000'000 Adena<br />1x Kaliel's Energy<br />70x Giant's Energy|

\* All adena costs can change based on castle tax

*Talisman of Insanity* can be obtained also by compouding *Talisman of Longing*.
*Heavenly Talisman* can be obtained **only** by compounding 2x *Talisman of Insanity*.

|Slot 1|Slot 2|Chance|Success|
|--|--|--|--|
|Talisman of Longing|Talisman of Longing|7%|Talisman of Insanity|
|Talisman of Longing|Energy of Longing|7%|Talisman of Insanity|
|Talisman of Insanity|Talisman of Insanity|4%|Heavenly Talisman|

### Sealing
All seed talismans, except Heavenly, can't be traded.
Talisman of Insanity and Talisman of Longing can be sealed via NPC Seed Talisman Manager, to make them tradable.

|Item|Cost|
|--|--|
|Sealed Talisman of Longing|Talisman of Longing<br />72x Destructive Energy<br />200x Giant's Energy|
|Sealed Talisman of Insanity|Talisman of Insanity<br />100x Destructive Energy<br />900x Giant's Energy|
