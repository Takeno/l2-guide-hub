# Talismans

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  This article is not completed yet.
</Callout>

Talismans in Lineage 2 are powerful artifacts that bestow unique bonuses and enhancements to your character.\
You can equip up to 6 talismans and you need a [Bracelet](#bracelets) with available slots.

Effects of two identical talisman do not stack, the higher-level talisman takes precedence.
