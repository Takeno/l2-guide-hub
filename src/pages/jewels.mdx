# Jewels and brooch

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
  This article is not completed yet.
</Callout>


Jewels are special gems required to gain powerful active and/or passive skills while wearing a brooch. There are 16 types of jewel.

Jewels have 3 upgrading steps: regular, greater and superior. Regular and greater jewels are divided into 5 levels; superior jewels are divided in 10 levels.

A maximum of 6 stones can be inserted into a brooch (depending on the brooch) and effects from same type don't stack.
