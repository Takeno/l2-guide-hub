# Jewels

Jewels are special gems required to gain powerful active and/or passive skills while wearing a [brooch](/brooch). There are 16 types of jewel.

Jewels have 3 upgrading steps: regular, greater and superior. Regular and greater jewels are divided into 5 levels; superior jewels are divided in 10 levels.

A maximum of 6 stones can be inserted into a brooch (depending on the brooch) and effects from same type don't stack.

## Getting Jewels

Random regular jewels Lv. 1 can be obtained by [Ultimate Jewelry Box](https://l2central.info/main/items/28470.html) that can be purchased in Einhasad's Store for 30 Einhasad's Golden Coins. In addition, [Jewel Grinder](https://l2central.info/main/items/28364.html) is obtained by the box.

Greater jewels can be obtained as loot from bosses.

Talking with Shadai in Heine, jewels lv. 1 can be compounded with Jewel Grinder to obtain [La Vie En Rose's Jewel Powder](https://l2central.info/main/items/28359.html). This powder is used to seal regular jewels Lv. 3 and Lv. 4 into tradable packs. But it is also used to get a regular jewel Lv. 3 or Lv. 4 pack from a pack Lv. 1.

|Required|Obtain|
|--|--|
|15x La Vie En Rose's Jewel Powder<br />1x \<regular type\> Lv.3|\<same type\> Jewelry Box Lv. 3|
|90x La Vie En Rose's Jewel Powder<br />1x \<regular type\> Lv.4|\<same type\> Jewelry Box Lv. 4|
|30x La Vie En Rose's Jewel Powder<br />1x La Vie En Rose's \<regular type\> Pack|\<same type\> Jewelry Box Lv. 3|
|200x La Vie En Rose's Jewel Powder<br />1x La Vie En Rose's \<regular type\> Pack|\<same type\> Jewelry Box Lv. 4|




## Upgrading

Regular jewels can be compounded with another of same type and level to get the higher level.

>	E.g: Ruby Lv. 2 + Ruby Lv. 2 = Ruby Lv. 3 or Ruby Lv. 2

All jewels can be compounded with energies too but in case of failure the jewel level is reset to Lv. 1

>	E.g: Greater Ruby Lv. 3 + Jewel Energy = Greater Ruby Lv. 4 or Greater Ruby Lv. 1
