## Items involved

[Forgotten Spellbook Chapter 1](https://l2wiki.com/main/items/48876.html) are used to learn skills and to craft higher books.
How to get spellbook:
- Einhasad's Store for **215 coins**
- [Water Dragon's Elite Supplies](https://l2wiki.com/main/items/81758.html) from [[Ravaged Innadril]]
- Event's chests

From 6 to 10: 25x Forgotten Spellbook Chapter 1\
From 11 to 20: 50x Forgotten Spellbook Chapter 2\
From 21 to 30: 50x Forgotten Spellbook Chapter 3
