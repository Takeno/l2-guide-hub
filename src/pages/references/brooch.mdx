# Brooch

The brooch is the accessory that allows to use [jewels](/references/jewels) when equipped.

Each brooch allow a different number of slots for jewels and have different effects.

## La Vie En Rose's Brooch

The main brooch is La Vie En Rose's Brooch which have 7 different levels. Brooch lv. 1 to lv. 5 can't be enchanted nor augmented. Enhanced brooch can be enchanted up to +5; Superior Brooch can be enchanted up to +10.


|Name|Description|Passive trigger|
|--|--|--|
|La Vie En Rose's Brooch Lv. 1|Unlocks 4 jewel slots. Inventory slots +12.||
|La Vie En Rose's Brooch Lv. 2|Unlocks 5 jewel slots. Inventory slots +12.||
|La Vie En Rose's Brooch Lv. 3|Unlocks 6 jewel slots. Inventory slots +12.||
|La Vie En Rose's Brooch Lv. 4|Unlocks 6 jewel slots. Inventory slots +12. CHA +1||
|La Vie En Rose's Brooch Lv. 5|Unlocks 6 jewel slots. Inventory slots +12, all basic stats +2, Max CP +12%.||
|Enhanced La Vie En Rose's Brooch|Unlocks 6 jewel slots. Inventory slots +12 + additional enhacement effects|With a certain chance, applies La Vie En Rose's Blessing for 15 sec. (P./ M. Atk. +10%) after a basic attack or skill use.|
|Superior La Vie En Rose's Brooch|Unlocks 6 jewel slots. Inventory slots +12 + additional enhacement effects|With a certain chance, applies La Vie En Rose's Shining Blessing for 15 sec. (P./ M. Atk. +15%) after a basic attack or skill use.|
